Banana Bread

Ingredients

  • Ripe bananas
  • Flour, sugar
  • Eggs
  • Butter
  • Baking soda, salt
  • Vanilla

Instructions

  1. Mash bananas; mix with sugar, eggs, melted butter, and vanilla.
  2. Fold in dry ingredients gently.
  3. Pour into loaf pan; bake at 175°C (350°F) for 50–60 min.
  4. Cool and slice.
