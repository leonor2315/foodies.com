Lentil Soup

Ingredients

  • 1 cup dried lentils
  • Onion, carrot, celery
  • Garlic, cumin
  • Vegetable stock
  • Salt, pepper, lemon

Instructions

  1. Sauté aromatics in oil until softened.
  2. Add lentils and stock; simmer until tender.
  3. Season and finish with a squeeze of lemon.
  4. Serve warm with crusty bread.
