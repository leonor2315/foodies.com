Spicy Chicken Wings

Ingredients

  • Chicken wings
  • Hot sauce
  • Butter
  • Garlic powder, paprika
  • Salt, pepper

Instructions

  1. Season wings; bake or air-fry until crispy.
  2. Melt butter and mix with hot sauce and spices.
  3. Toss wings in sauce; return to heat briefly.
  4. Serve with ranch or blue cheese.
