Vegan Chocolate Cake

Ingredients

  • Flour, sugar, cocoa powder
  • Baking soda, salt
  • Plant milk
  • Vegetable oil
  • Vanilla, vinegar

Instructions

  1. Whisk dry ingredients together.
  2. Combine with wet ingredients; mix until smooth.
  3. Pour into greased pan; bake at 175°C (350°F) for 30–35 min.
  4. Cool, frost if desired, and serve.
