Chickpea Curry

Ingredients

  • Chickpeas (canned or cooked)
  • Onion, garlic, ginger
  • Tomatoes
  • Coconut milk
  • Spices: curry powder, cumin, turmeric

Instructions

  1. Sauté onion, garlic, and ginger.
  2. Add tomatoes and spices; simmer.
  3. Stir in chickpeas and coconut milk; cook until thick.
  4. Serve with rice and fresh cilantro.
