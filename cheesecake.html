Classic Cheesecake

Ingredients

  • Cream cheese
  • Sugar
  • Eggs
  • Vanilla
  • Graham cracker crust

Instructions

  1. Make crust and press into pan; bake briefly.
  2. Beat cream cheese, sugar, eggs, and vanilla.
  3. Pour filling over crust; bake at 160°C (325°F) until set.
  4. Cool and chill before slicing.
