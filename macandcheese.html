Creamy Mac & Cheese

Ingredients

  • Macaroni pasta
  • Butter, flour (for roux)
  • Milk
  • Cheddar cheese (and a little mozzarella)
  • Salt, pepper, paprika
  • Bread crumbs (optional topping)

Instructions

  1. Cook macaroni until al dente; drain.
  2. Make roux with butter and flour; whisk in milk to make a smooth sauce.
  3. Stir in cheeses, salt, pepper, paprika.
  4. Combine pasta with sauce; top with crumbs and bake 20 minutes at 180°C (350°F).
