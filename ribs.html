BBQ Ribs

Ingredients

  • Pork ribs
  • BBQ sauce
  • Dry rub (paprika, garlic powder, brown sugar)
  • Salt and pepper

Instructions

  1. Rub ribs with dry seasoning; marinate if possible.
  2. Bake low and slow at 150°C (300°F) until tender.
  3. Brush with BBQ sauce and finish on grill or broil.
  4. Rest and slice.
