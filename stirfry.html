Veggie Stir-Fry

Ingredients

  • Broccoli, bell peppers, carrots, snap peas
  • Soy sauce, ginger, garlic
  • Sesame oil
  • Optional: tofu or chicken

Instructions

  1. Heat sesame oil in a wok, add garlic and ginger.
  2. Stir-fry veggies on high heat until crisp-tender.
  3. Add soy sauce and toss; add protein if using.
  4. Serve over rice or noodles.
