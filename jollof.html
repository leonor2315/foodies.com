Jollof Rice

Ingredients

  • 2 cups long-grain rice (washed)
  • 1 cup tomato puree
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 bell peppers, blended
  • Garlic, ginger, curry powder, bay leaf
  • 2–3 cups chicken or vegetable stock
  • Salt, pepper, oil

Instructions

  1. Sauté onions, garlic, and ginger in oil until fragrant.
  2. Add blended peppers and tomato puree; cook down until thick and sweet.
  3. Season with curry, bay leaf, salt, and pepper.
  4. Stir in rice and stock; cover and cook on low until rice is tender.
  5. Fluff, adjust seasoning, and serve with grilled chicken or plantains.
