Garlic Butter Shrimp Pasta

Ingredients

  • 200g spaghetti
  • 300g shrimp, peeled
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 3 tbsp butter
  • Olive oil, salt, pepper, chili flakes
  • Lemon juice and parsley

Instructions

  1. Cook spaghetti according to package directions.
  2. Sauté garlic in butter and oil, add shrimp and cook until pink.
  3. Toss pasta with shrimp, season with salt, pepper, chili, and lemon.
  4. Finish with chopped parsley and serve warm.
