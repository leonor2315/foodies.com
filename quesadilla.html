Chicken Quesadilla

Ingredients

  • Flour tortillas
  • Cooked shredded chicken
  • Cheddar or mozzarella
  • Onion, bell pepper
  • Salsa and sour cream

Instructions

  1. Sauté onion and pepper; add chicken to warm through.
  2. Place mixture and cheese on tortillas; fold.
  3. Cook in a pan until golden and cheese melted.
  4. Slice and serve with salsa and sour cream.
