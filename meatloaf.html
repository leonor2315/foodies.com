Juicy Meatloaf

Ingredients

  • 700g ground beef
  • 1 cup bread crumbs
  • 2 eggs
  • Onion, garlic
  • Milk or stock (splash)
  • Ketchup or tomato paste
  • Salt, pepper, herbs

Instructions

  1. Mix beef, crumbs, eggs, onion, garlic, and seasoning; add splash of milk.
  2. Shape into loaf; place on lined baking tray.
  3. Spread ketchup on top.
  4. Bake at 180°C (350°F) for 55–65 minutes; rest 10 minutes.
