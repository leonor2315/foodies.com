Beef Stroganoff

Ingredients

  • Beef strips
  • Mushrooms
  • Onion, garlic
  • Beef stock
  • Sour cream
  • Mustard, paprika

Instructions

  1. Sear beef; set aside.
  2. Sauté onions and mushrooms; add garlic.
  3. Deglaze with stock; return beef and simmer.
  4. Stir in sour cream and mustard; serve over noodles.
