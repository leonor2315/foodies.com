Garlic Breadsticks

Ingredients

  • Pizza dough or breadstick dough
  • Butter
  • Garlic, parsley
  • Salt, parmesan (optional)

Instructions

  1. Roll dough into sticks and place on tray.
  2. Brush with garlic butter; sprinkle parsley.
  3. Bake at 200°C (400°F) until golden.
  4. Finish with parmesan if desired.
