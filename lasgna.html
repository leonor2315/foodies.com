Italian Lasagna

Ingredients

  • Lasagna sheets
  • 500g ground beef
  • Tomato sauce (marinara)
  • Onion, garlic
  • Ricotta, mozzarella, parmesan
  • Basil, oregano, salt, pepper

Instructions

  1. Sauté onion and garlic; brown beef. Season and add marinara; simmer.
  2. In a bowl, mix ricotta with parmesan, salt, pepper, and basil.
  3. Layer: sauce, sheets, ricotta mix, mozzarella. Repeat.
  4. Top with sauce and mozzarella; bake at 180°C (350°F) for 35–40 minutes.
  5. Rest 10 minutes before slicing.
