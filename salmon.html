Grilled Salmon

Ingredients

  • Salmon fillets
  • Olive oil
  • Lemon, garlic
  • Salt, pepper
  • Dill or parsley

Instructions

  1. Brush salmon with oil; season with salt, pepper, garlic.
  2. Grill skin-side down until nearly cooked through.
  3. Flip briefly; squeeze lemon and garnish with herbs.
  4. Serve with vegetables or rice.
